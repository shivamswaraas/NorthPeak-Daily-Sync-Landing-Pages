How It Works

Three steps to a meeting-free morning.

NorthPeak was built to be the quiet layer of your workflow — it never screams for attention, it just organises it.

01

Set your cadence and go.

Choose when you want updates — daily standups, weekly retros, or project-specific check-ins. SOP templates handle the structure so you get real data, not "I'm busy" fluff.

02

Team updates on their time.

Your team submits a 30-second async check-in directly in Slack or Google Workspace — no new tab, no new login, no context switch required.

03

You see the Blocker Map.

NorthPeak's analytics engine scans check-ins for patterns. If a task has been "In Progress" 3+ days without a commit, it's flagged. You see the fire before it burns the deadline.
